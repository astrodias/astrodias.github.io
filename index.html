About

Astrodias is a fictional 7 member idol unit from beyond the stars created with the intention of one day making an Otome game.
With a name that comes from the Latin words for "star" and "day", it's a unit that hopes to shine everyday of the week.

About Me

Hi! I'm 9bsoul! This project first started as a short story parody of idol and reverse harem anime back in 2018.
Years later, I decided to repurpose it into a visual novel and, after some research, I fell in love with both otome games and idols!
TBH I'm still honing my skills to turn this into a real game, so cheer me on if you're interested, okay~
